About Company

Enki Cure is a medtech startup that develops medical hardware and software devices. Our vision is to give freedom and independence to patients suffering from dementia and to their caregivers. We are a highly experienced team belonging to healthcare and product and technology development. Enki cure has developed a suite of non-intrusive devices especially designed keeping in mind the special needs of dementia patients and of their caregivers.
